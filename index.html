Biography

Now, I am a PhD student at LIGUM at Université de Montréal and supervised by Prof. Bernhard Thomaszewski. I received M.Eng. and B.Eng. degree from Shanghai University, P.R.China, and supervised by Prof. Youdong Ding and Dr. Dongjin Huang

My research interests are computer graphics, numerical simulation and optimization, and as well as physically-based simulations.
